About TaxiGo

Connecting passengers with trusted drivers since 2024.

Our Story

TaxiGo was founded to make urban transportation in Sri Lanka simpler, safer, and more affordable. We combine modern technology with a network of professional drivers to deliver rides you can count on.

Whether you need a quick trip across town, an airport transfer, or a premium vehicle for a special event, TaxiGo is here to get you there comfortably and on time.

Mission

Provide safe, accessible transport for everyone.

Vision

Become Sri Lanka's most trusted ride platform.

Values

Safety, honesty, and customer-first service.

Our Team

A dedicated team of engineers, operations staff, and support agents work around the clock to keep TaxiGo running smoothly for riders and drivers alike.
