Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick, comprehensive answers to our system procedures, travel fares, and ride category dispatch queries.

How do I book a ride?

Create an account, sign in, and go to **Book Ride** in your passenger portal. Enter your pickup and drop-off locations, date, target time, and vehicle tier, then complete your payment.

How is the fare calculated?

Fares start at an LKR 500 base rate plus an estimated distance charge of 120 LKR per kilometer. Premium SUVs and XL vehicles include modifiers, and active **Premium VIP Members** receive a 10% cash discount automatically!

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. You can cancel any confirmed booking at any time directly from your passenger dashboard hub before your target pickup dispatch schedule begins.

What payment methods are accepted?

We support secure simulated credit card checkout, direct local banking options, and mobile pay gateways. You can configure your preferred payment category in your user profile parameters.

Is TaxiGo available outside Colombo?

We currently focus on Colombo Metropolitan and airport shuttle routes. For specialized inter-district, long-duration bookings, please submit an inquiry form.
