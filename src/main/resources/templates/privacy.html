Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

TaxiGo collects personal information (name, email, phone) when you register and book rides. This data is stored securely and used only to provide our premium passenger transport services.

We do not sell or lease your data to any third parties. Location telemetry coordinates are used solely for real-time ride dispatch matching and are not retained beyond trip completion.

You may request passenger account deletion at any time directly from your user dashboard profile page parameters.

For privacy enquiries, please contact privacy@taxigo.lk.
