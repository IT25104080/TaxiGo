Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

TaxiGo collects personal information (name, email, phone) when you register and book rides. This data is stored securely and used only to provide our services.

We do not sell your data to third parties. Location data is used solely for ride matching and is not retained beyond trip completion.

You may request account deletion at any time from your profile page.

For privacy enquiries, contact privacy@taxigo.lk.
